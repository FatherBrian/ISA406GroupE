Miami University Varsity Esports

In 2016 Miami University established the first Division 1 Esports program

Miami University Esports supports teams in the major competitive games such as "Counter Strike : Global Offensive", "Overwatch", "Rocket League", "League of Legends", and "Hearthstone". Along with this Miami University takes pride in supporting games through our groundbreaking hatchery program that helps support athletes in games like "Super Smash Bros".

The Miami University Redhawks are a founding team for division 1 college Esports. By getting ahead of the curve Miami University has been able to fully develop comptetive teams, and get recognized for their success at a national level. Winning national championships and getting recognized in college rankings has made Miami University one of the biggest names in college varsity Esports.

Katherine Liu of Lineups.com
